Kesar Dryfruit Milk

Description :

This Healthy Saffron Milk as the name mentions is a warm, thickened milk-based drink that is infused with the flavors of saffron and is enriched with dry fruits. Make this 3-ingredients Indian Kesar Milk for a soothing winter drink. Here is how to make it.

Ingridients :

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Natural Flavour
  • Custard Powder
  • Cream
  • Saffron
  • Dryfruit

Steps :

  1. Heat the milk fully.
  2. Add Sffron in the Milk.
  3. Add Cream while heating.
  4. Add Sugar
  5. Add Custard Powder and Natural Flavour.
  6. Cut the dryfruits like Almond, Cashew Nut, Hazel Nut and Pista into small pieces.
  7. Premix the dryfruit into the milk.
  8. Enjoy the Milk and feel the richness.
